Presidents of India

The President of India is the head of state of india and the commander-in-chief of the Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is referred as the first citizen of india. Although vested with these powers by the Constitution of India, the position is largely a ceremonial one and executive powers are de facto exercised by the Prime Minister.
